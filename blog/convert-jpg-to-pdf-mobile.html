No app download required. Convert your photos to PDF directly from your phone's browser.

Introduction

Scanning documents usually requires a heavy app, but with ToolZilla, you can convert JPG to PDF on mobile with just a few taps. It works perfectly on both iOS and Android.

Step-by-Step Mobile Guide

  • Open Browser: Go to ToolZilla.com on Chrome, Safari, or your preferred mobile browser.
  • Select Photos: Tap the upload area and pick images from your camera roll.
  • Arrange: Drag your thumbnails to reorder them before converting.
  • Save: Tap the convert button and download the PDF directly to your device.
